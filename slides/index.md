# Lecture Slides

All slide decks are compiled from [Marp](https://marp.app/) Markdown and served as self-contained HTML presentations.

**How to use:**
- Click **▶ View** to open the slide deck in a new browser tab — use arrow keys or spacebar to advance slides.
- Click **⬇ PDF** to download a static PDF version for offline reading or printing.
- On the **▶ View** page, press `F` for full-screen or `P` for the presenter notes view.
